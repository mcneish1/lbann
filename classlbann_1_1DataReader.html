Given that each model has to have at least one reader, what is the minimum stride

By default the last mini-batch is a full one

The first reader that doesn't have an extra mini batch gets the partial batch

Note that comm->get_model_rank() + comm->get_rank_in_model() is not equivalent to comm->get_world_rank() from a parallel I/O perspective Given the data readers model rank, how many models have a higher rank

By default the last stride of each reader is part of a regular (full) round

The last mini-batch may be partial and thus may have a smaller stride
